How We Play

  • 💷 £10 buy-in per player, with rebuys usually available two hours after play begins.
  • 🎯 Each player starts with 5,000 chips.
  • 📆 We play one game per month, 8-handed—first come, first served.
  • 🪑 Players who vote 'Yes' after the 8-player limit are added to a first-reserve list.
  • 🃏 We follow a “show one, show all” rule—if you reveal a card to any player, you must show it to everyone.
